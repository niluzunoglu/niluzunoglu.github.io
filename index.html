About Me

Hello and welcome to my website! My name is Nil, and I'm a software engineer with a focus on backend development and artificial intelligence systems.

I recently graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering from Ankara University in June 2022. On this website, you'll find examples of my work, including projects I've completed and code I've written.

Please feel free to contact me if you have any questions or opportunities for collaboration. Thank you for visiting my site, and I look forward to connecting with you!

Work

Currently, I am working as a backend developer at KutuAI, where I use Python as programming language and Django as framework.

I gained valuable experience as an intern and junior software engineer at Sparta, a cyber security company where I worked on web applications and web application security.

I'm always eager to learn new technologies and take on new challenges, and I'm passionate about building scalable and efficient systems that can handle complex data processing and analysis.

Projects

I'm proud to have been a part of a team that made a real-world impact through our project on "Sepsis Detection and Treatment using Machine Learning." Our project was the first of its kind in Turkey and aimed to improve the detection and treatment of sepsis using machine learning. Thanks to our efforts, we were able to shed light on the importance of early diagnosis and timely intervention in treating this life-threatening condition. I'm grateful to have had the opportunity to work with my team on this challenging project and to contribute to improving healthcare in Turkey. This project reinforced my belief in the power of technology and data analysis to make a positive impact in people's lives. I look forward to continuing to explore the potential of machine learning and artificial intelligence in healthcare and other fields.
